Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Welcome to MOD STORE ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Android application. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in various ways. The information we collect may include:

  • GitHub Profile Data: When you sign in with GitHub, we access your public GitHub profile information, including your username, avatar URL, and GitHub user ID. We do not access your repositories, private data, or any information beyond your public profile.
  • Favorites & Library Data: The list of apps you mark as favorites is stored and associated with your GitHub user ID to enable synchronization across devices.
  • Usage Information: We may collect non-personally identifiable information about how you use the app, such as search queries and pages visited, to improve app performance.
  • Device Information: Basic device information (Android version, device model) may be collected for crash reporting and compatibility purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and personalize the MOD STORE experience.
  • Sync your library and preferences across devices linked to your GitHub account.
  • Display your GitHub avatar and username within the app.
  • Analyze app usage to identify bugs and improve features.
  • Respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues.

3. GitHub OAuth & Token Storage

When you sign in with GitHub, an OAuth access token is generated by GitHub and stored securely on your device. This token is used solely to identify you and retrieve your public profile. We store the minimum data necessary to maintain your session. You can revoke this authorization at any time from your GitHub Authorized Apps settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with trusted service providers necessary to operate the app (e.g., hosting, analytics) under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Retention

We retain your library and favorites data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your GitHub-linked data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. OAuth tokens are stored using Android's secure credential storage mechanisms where available.

7. Children's Privacy

MOD STORE is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us via the official GitHub repository.
